DUNJOURN 0.0.1

A gradual conversion of my unfinished roguelike project, Flamecant, into an experimental social media platform where users have dungeons instead of profiles.

Don't expect much yet, I'm still working on setting up the minimal static version of this concept. For an extremely loose plan or outline, see /outline.

-------------| CONTROLS | --------------

  • movement controls are no longer numpad only!
    • new: you can use qweasdzxc like the numpad for movement!
    • what this means is imagine the keys Q W E / A S D / Z X C are on a compass, with S at the center;
    • each letter makes you move in its corresponding compass direction, except for S, which makes you wait for one turn in place!
  • for numpad controls, it works like old school roguelikes: the compass is centered on 5.
  • to attack an enemy, move into its tile!
  • when using numpad don't forget to turn on numlock!
  • p to pick up items in your tile
  • i to open inventory
  • use the letter index next to the menu item you want to select!
  • escape should back you out of any menus, but it's possible I missed something.
  • cursor/selector and mouse options under consideration!
  • on multi-select menus (like picking up multiple items from a tile):
  • hit all the indices for desired menu items to change the "-" into a "+" and then hit enter/return to pick them up!
  • comma descends downward staircases (">""); period ascends upward ones ("<"
